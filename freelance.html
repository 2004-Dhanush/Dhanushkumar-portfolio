Freelancing

I conducted a workshop titled "Sketch to Reality" at Surya Engineering College, Erode, where I trained over 100 students on the fundamentals of UI/UX design using Figma. The workshop focused on guiding students through the complete design process, from initial sketching to creating fully interactive digital prototypes.

View Certificate

Key Topics

UI/UX design principles, user-centric design, wireframing, prototyping, and real-time collaboration using Figma. Students engaged in hands-on activities, worked in teams, and presented their final designs. They gained practical experience and confidence in their design skills. The workshop received highly positive feedback for being interactive, informative, and inspiring.
